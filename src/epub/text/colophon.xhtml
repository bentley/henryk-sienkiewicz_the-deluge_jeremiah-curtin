Colophon

The Deluge
was published in 1886 by
Henryk Sienkiewicz.
It was translated from Polish in 1891 by
Jeremiah Curtin.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Robin Whittleton,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2011 by
Charles Bowen
for
Project Gutenberg (volumes 1 and 2)
and on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive (volumes 1 and 2).

The cover page is adapted from
Departure of John III Sobieski and Marysieńka Sobieska from Wilanów,
a painting completed in 1897 by
Józef Brandt.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
